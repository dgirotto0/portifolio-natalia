About me

I am a 22-year-old multi-artist currently in my third semester of fashion design at Faculdade Santa Marcelina. My academic journey was enriched by a six-month exchange program in England, where I was honored with a merit medal for my academic performance. During this time, I discovered my passion for fashion by volunteering at an NGO that sold clothes to help children with learning disabilities such as dyslexia, dyscalculia, and dysgraphia. This experience not only reinforced my love for design but also showed me the positive impact fashion can have on people's lives.

As a communicative, creative person who works well in a team, I love to draw and am constantly seeking to improve my skills and learn new things. I am fluent in English and have an intermediate level of Spanish, which allows me to navigate different cultures and perspectives with ease. I am proficient in Corel Draw, Illustrator, and Photoshop, always striving to enhance my abilities within digital platforms. I navigate between art and fashion, utilizing the academic space to explore and experiment with the goal of communicating messages in their purest form. I am always ready for new challenges and opportunities that can expand my knowledge and contribute to my personal and professional growth.
